Detecting Building Defects using AI

Building defects can be costly and dangerous. They can lead to structural problems, leaks, and even collapses. But with the help of a machine learning
model, you can now identify building defects early on.

Our model was trained on a dataset of images of buildings with and without defects. It can now accurately predict whether a building has any defects.
To use our model, simply upload an image of a building. The model will then predict whether the building has any defects.
If it does, you will be given a list of possible defects.

This information can help you take steps to repair the defects and prevent further damage.
